Gon Freecs

Anime Character from Hunter × Hunter

Gon Freecss is the main protagonist of Hunter × Hunter, created by Yoshihiro is a cheerful and Togashi. He determined boy who sets out to become a Hunter in order to find his father, Ging Freecss. As an Enhancer, Gon uses his Nen ability called Jajanken, a rock-paper-scissors–based attack that reflects his straightforward personality. Despite his innocence, he can become extremely intense and ruthless when protecting the people he cares about.

Passed the Hunter Exam

At just 11 years old, Gon passed the extremely dangerous Hunter Exam on his first attempt — something very few candidates accomplish.

Defeated Strong Opponents

Beat Hanzo during the Hunter Exam (mentally endured torture without giving up). Defeated Genthru ("The Bomber") in the Greed Island arc using strategy and raw determination.

Adult Transformation

During the Chimera Ant arc, Gon made a terrifying Nen vow that temporarily transformed him into an adult version of himself, granting immense power.
